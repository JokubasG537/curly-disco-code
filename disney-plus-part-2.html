Frequently Asked Questions

What is Disney+? Disney+ is the streaming home for entertainment from Disney, Pixar, Marvel, Star Wars, National Geographic, Star and more.
What can I watch on Disney+?
  • Exclusive new original movies and series
  • Timeless classics and recent hits from Walt Disney Animation Studios
  • An ever-growing collection of general entertainment, spanning comedy, drama, true crime and more
  • Heart-warming storytelling for all ages from the masterminds at Pixar
  • The entire Star Wars Saga and much of the Marvel Cinematic Universe
  • Documentaries from National Geographic, unscripted series, and so much more
How much does Disney+ cost? How much does Disney+ cost?
  • Premium (€11.99/month or €119.90/year): Up to 4K UHD & HDR video. 4 concurrent streams. Downloads on up to 10 devices. Up to Dolby Atmos audio. Ad-free movies and series.
  • Standard (€8.99/month or €89.90/year): Up to 1080p Full HD video. 2 concurrent streams. Downloads on up to 10 devices. Up to 5.1 audio. Ad-free movies and series.
What devices are supported? Disney+ supports mobile devices, web browsers, game consoles, set-top boxes, and smart TVs. Click here for the full list.
Is there any commitment when signing up for Disney+? There are no commitments and you can cancel at any time, effective at the end of your current payment period. Just follow the below 5 easy steps:
  1. Go to www.disneyplus.com and log in
  2. Select your Profile
  3. Select Account
  4. Select Cancel Subscription
  5. Select Complete Cancellation to confirm
